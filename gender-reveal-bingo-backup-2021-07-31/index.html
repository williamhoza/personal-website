Gender Reveal Bingo

This is a tool for setting up a gender reveal party. At the party, guests divide into two teams ("Team Girl" and "Team Boy") and play a game of bingo. The game is rigged: if the baby is a girl, someone on Team Girl will win; if the baby is a boy, someone on Team Boy will win.

How to use this tool:

  1. When you are with your doctor (or someone else who knows the sex of your baby), load this webpage on a phone.
  2. Have your doctor (or whoever) press a button on that webpage indicating the sex of the baby.
  3. That webpage will generate a custom link that you can use to print bingo cards before the party and run the game on the day of the party.

Note that the parents do not need to learn the sex of the baby prior to the gender reveal party.
